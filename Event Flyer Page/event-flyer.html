BloomFest 2025: A Celebration of Flowers & Flavors

Experience a vibrant fusion of colors, scents, and tastes at BloomFest 2025!

Celebrate the beauty of nature with stunning flower displays, lively performances, and mouth-watering delicacies from the best food trucks in town.

📅 When: March 22–24, 2025 📍 Where: City Central Park, Manila

Festival Highlights

  • 🌺 Grand Flower Parade with over 50 floral floats
  • 🎶 Live music performances from local and national artists
  • 🎨 Art corner featuring floral painting and pottery workshops
  • 📸 Instagram-worthy flower dome installations
  • 🎭 Cultural dance and theater shows

Special Guests

  • 🎤 Clara Mendoza – Award-winning singer-songwriter
  • 🎷 The Manila Jazz Ensemble – Renowned jazz group
  • 🌿 Chef Antonio Ruiz – Celebrity chef specializing in organic cuisine

Food Trucks

  • 🍔 Burger Bliss – Gourmet burgers & loaded fries
  • 🌮 Taco Fiesta – Authentic Mexican tacos & nachos
  • 🍦 Sweet Swirl – Artisan ice cream & milkshakes
  • 🍜 Noodle Street – Asian noodle bowls & dumplings
